Warner Bros Studio Tour London

    No text

Overview

Located on the outskirts of London, Warner Bros. Studio is home to a treasure trove of Harry Potter film sets. Step on to authentic sets, discover the magic behind spellbinding special effects and explore the behind-the-scenes secrets of the Harry Potter film series. Tread the original stone floor of the iconic Hogwarts Great Hall, encounter animatronic creatures, wander down Diagon Alley.

6-7 hours
