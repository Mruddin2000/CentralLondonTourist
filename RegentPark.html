Overview

Regent's Park is one of the Royal Parks of London. Originally a royal hunting ground, this scenic park is a popular recreation area offering an artificial lake, tennis courts, cricket ground, children's playgrounds and the London Zoo, which lies on the north side of the park. It occupies high ground in north-west Inner London, it split between the City of Westminster and the Borough of Camden.
