Overview

Take in some of London’s most iconic sights from the deck of a City Cruises river tour boat. Sail past attractions such as the London Eye. City Cruises is a cruise vessel operator in England with operations in London, Poole and York. It is a subsidiary of Hornblower Cruises. City Cruises was incorporated in 1986. City Cruises is now the largest river sightseeing company on the River Thames.
