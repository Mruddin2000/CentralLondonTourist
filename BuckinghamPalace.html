Overview

Buckingham Palace is the London home and the administrative center of the British royal family. This is an important site of ceremonial and political affairs in the United Kingdom. Buckingham Palace is recognised around the world as the focus of national and royal celebrations as well as the backdrop to the regular Changing the Guard ceremony.
