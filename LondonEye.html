    No text

Overview

The London eye is a very well known structure in London. It is a very massive ferries wheel that sits on the south bank of the river thames. A trip on the London Eye is truly captivating, a ‘must-do’ and has been London’s number one visitor experience for over a decade. There are mesmerising 360-degree views of the capital laid out before you - London’s most famous landmarks such as Windsor Castle.

1-2 hours
