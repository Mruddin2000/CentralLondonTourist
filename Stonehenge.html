Overview

Explore the beautiful Georgian city of Bath and its ancient Roman Baths*, and uncover the mystery behind Stonehenge - the world's most famous Neolithic site. One of the most important survivals of prehistoric England, Stonehenge consists of a group of huge rough-cut stones, some more than 20 feet high, arranged in two concentric circles. Stonehenge is regarded as a British cultural icon.
